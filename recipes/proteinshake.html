Protein Shake

Description

This protein shake is sure to get you gluten free nutrition.


Enjoy this shake plain or blended with a banana.

  • 2 cups water or beverage of choice
  • 45g protein powder
  • 10g chia seeds

Steps

  1. Pour in the water or beverage
  2. Add the protein powder and chia seeds
  3. Shake thoroughly for 15 seconds and enjoy

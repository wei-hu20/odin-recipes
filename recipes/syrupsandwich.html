Syrup Sandwich

Description

A classic of the American ruling class and bon-vivant, this syrup sandwich recipe straight from the delicatessen is sure to impress.


Make sure to brush your teeth after eating and drink a lot of water.

  • 2 slices processed white bread
  • 75g honey, syrup, or fruit jam of choice
  • Side beverage of choice

Steps

  1. Put the bread on a plate
  2. Apply honey/syrup/jam
  3. Close sandwich and enjoy
